Machine Learning

Time series, decision trees and neural nets. All models have a place, but the art is finding the right one. I enjoy the challenge that comes with Data Science in general and love the chance to improve my skills.

Advanced Data visualization

Whether you need to create KPIs for production or simulate future outcomes, I can create a visual for your use case. With extensive experience in Power BI and SQL, just about anything can be built to make data driven decisions.

Data Consultancy

Data is underutilized unless the organization knows how to use it. By building a data centric culture one can greatly improve desirable outcomes and simplify a myriad of processes. From VPs to CxOs, I have worked with every level of the organization to achieve these goals.
